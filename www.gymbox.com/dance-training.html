Introducing Out The Box: Gymbox Online dance classes you can do at home

FIND ONLINE DANCE CLASSES AT GYMBOX

When we founded Gymbox we wanted to make this a place that everyone could find their workout, however they like to move. Yes, that means having the best gear and massive gainz. But we also wanted to offer the best dance classes in the capital – and there’s not many gyms where you’ll find a fully-blown dance school thrown in as part of the deal. Now, you can hit up our carefully choreographed routines with some of London’s best professional dancers. Where? Out The Box, of course.

You may know us for its nightclub-inspired London locations, epic playlists and all-round party vibes. But now, Out The Box is your brand new online platform that gives you access to a whole world of heart-racing, sweat-inducing, and totally unique online classes streamed to your TV, laptop, phone … or wherever you want to train.

With fitness focused across five different categories, we pride ourselves on making sure there’s something for everyone, whenever they need it. But for those looking to make some serious moves, there’s only one way to go – our Rhythm section. There, you’ll find some of our most popular dance classes to try from your home studio, in the park… or who knows, maybe one day we’ll be able to hit dancefloors again.

If you’ve been looking for online dance workouts to do at home, you’re in just the right place. You might want to learn to dance, enjoy live classes in your living room or just like to enjoy a virtual dance. We offer live sessions you can do at home while watching our expert in the dance studio. Gymbox has been leading the way when it comes to creative classes since day one, and Out The Box gives you the same experience with London’s best dance instructors, at the touch of a button. With new content from a broad range of dance styles being uploaded online every single week, we’ll help you find your feet whatever your level, from beginners to advanced. Keep reading to find out more about our online dance classes, our amazing instructors and how you can sign up to Out The Box. But first, let’s get you better acquainted with London’s most exciting gym… Gymbox.

Founded in 2003 in a worn-out West End car park, Gymbox has been a challenger of the status quo since day one. Setting out with a vision to disrupt the fitness industry, create unique training experiences and launch the most unique timetable the capital had ever seen, it’s a club known to be anything but your average kind of gym. It was among the first to introduce full-size boxing rings, nightly resident DJs, and led the way when it comes to kit.

To say Gymbox is EXTREMELY well equipped is a bit of an understatement. With state-of-the-art cardio equipment, stunning studio spaces, whopping free weights areas and super-clean changing rooms, members can’t get enough of the place. We’re constantly developing and evolving our huge timetable, welcoming in new concepts and styles within our Rhythm class category and beyond. Now, we’re bringing the very best dance classes in London online, and we can’t wait to share them with you.

TYPES OF ONLINE WORKOUT CLASSES

We’re raising the bar in stay at home workouts. Out The Box is your new online platform where we have redefined the best home workouts with the best of our creative classes coming direct to you from Gymbox, wherever you’re training right now.

Just like in club, we’ve got online workout classes ranging from holistic to sadistic, with 45- and 30-minute sessions across five different categories. With new content being uploaded each week, featured online workouts are spotlighted on the homepage for anyone looking to try something new.

By signing up, you get full access to the lot – the biggest issue is deciding where to start:

Strength

Nail your technique and you’ll get some proper sexy results. Focusing on strength, stability, power, mobility and flexibility, build your expertise and ability across the board.

Online workout classes include: Badass, Bodyweight Bandits, Functional Bodybuilding, Gymnastic Conditioning, Reppin’, Swingers Club, Thunder, WOD Squad

Sweat

HIIT. Circuit. Pure, puddle-inducing cardio. These classes are designed to get you sweating more than you ever thought possible as you chase that cheeky endorphin high.

Online workout classes include: Drill Sergeant, Escalate, Metcon, Sweat to the Beat, Swingers, Whiplash

Fight

Smash the techniques of combat styles from around the world. With top trainers and boxing coaches bossing it in your corner, there’s no title out of reach.

Online workout classes include: Killer Combat, Ultimate Fit

Holistic

Connect with your body and realign your mind in our holistic series: the classes that work on imbalances and fixes your focus for whatever life (or Gymbox) throws at you.

Online workout classes include: Active Beast, Ashtanga Yoga, Contortion, Hatha Yoga, Pilates, Rehab, Rollin’ With My Yogis, Vinyasa Flow Yoga, Yin Yoga

Rhythm

Whether you’re throwing shapes on the regular or want to ditch your dancefloor dread, these classes combine high-energy choreography, toning and tips to nail a range of styles.

Online workout classes include: Beats on Pointe, Callback, No Holds Barre, Shway, Tribal Queens

TYPES OF ONLINE DANCE CLASSES

If you’ve ever thought that dance doesn’t give you a ‘proper’ workout, you may need to reconsider. As well as improving the endurance of your muscles, shooting your heart rate up and improving stamina; regular dance classes could be just the thing you need to keep your body working harder for longer without getting tired.

The Dance category provides plenty of online classes to keep anyone busy. As any Gymbox member will tell you, we pride ourselves on making our clubs as inclusive as possible. That means it doesn’t matter whether you’ve got two left feet or moves that would put Beyonce to shame – our classes are totally accessible to all. So, what will it be?

BEATS ON POINTE

Rebel against the norm and chaseé to the sound of the street. A barre conditioning and rhythmic dance class to the sickest hip hop tracks.

If you’re new to barre, we can’t wait to surprise you with the intensity of this workout. As well as helping to strengthen and tone your muscles bulk-free, Barre is known to have a huge range of benefits – from improving your cardiovascular endurance and metabolism (oh hey, calorie burn) to boosting your bone density.

CALLBACK

Live for the dancefloor? Our music-video inspired commercial class is the place to choreograph killer routines open to dancers of every level. Work the studio with high-detail, high-energy moves to the freshest chart releases. Come shake whatever you’ve got.

NO HOLDS BARRE

Don’t let the tutu clichés fool you: Barre is the tough ballet workout that’ll hand you your derrière on a plate. Combining dynamic and static movements, isometric holds, core strengthening and stretching, this is a class that keeps you on your toes – giving you a longer, leaner, stronger body and improved postural awareness.

SHWAY

Got sh*tloads of sass and don’t know what to do with it? Step this Shway. Strutting straight out of the 70s underground club scene, this full body dance class styles footwork, hips and chest isolations with disco, house and soul to express your most fearless self. Your arms better get ready for the shapes they’re about to throw.

SWEAT TO THE BEAT

Whether you’re a slave to the rhythm or addicted to bass, this catchy little conditioning class is guaranteed to get peak fitness pouring from every pore. This is bodyweight endurance to a beat, challenging your strength, sculpting your figure and making music pulse through every muscle. The floor is yours.

TRIBAL QUEENS

When bellydance meets trap, hip hop and dubstep you get the ultimate dance workout. Learn to blend bellydance isolations and waves with popping and locking.

As a full body workout, bellydancing is an incredibly effective (not to mention sassy AF) way to spice up your training routine. With big calorie burn and middle-strengthening moves, the slow and deliberate moves will keep parts of your body engaged for longer periods of time than your average reps. That means great small muscle concentration, consistent cardio and a cracking core workout. What’s not to like?

HOW TO SIGN UP FOR OUR ONLINE WORKOUT CLASSES

It really couldn’t be easier to access the dozens of online workouts on Out The Box. Simply head to the website and create your account – it only takes a few clicks and a couple of details. Once you’re all set up, you’ve got all the digital classes you could ever want, ready to try at home.

If you’re new to online workouts and want to see if they’re your thing, we offer a free trial before you decide to commit. Once you’ve decided (and we’re sure you’ll want to stick around) you have the option to create a monthly subscription for £10 a month, or an annual subscription for £80. If you decide to pay as you go, you’ve got the option to cancel at any time – meaning there really is no reason to try!

When it comes to finding your next online dance class, you just need to head to the Rhythm section of the homepage. Once you’re there, you can navigate through our huge catalogue of dance workouts – from No Holds Barre and Callback all thr way through to Shway. It’s all about trying out different things and seeing what works for you, both in the style of dance and the type of instructor who’ll take you through it. Every Gymbox instructor is a true individual – and our online dance teachers are no exception. The more you try, the more you’ll see that!
